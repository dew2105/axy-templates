## Research Approach

### Investigation Process
1. Clarify the research question and scope
2. Identify reliable sources and data points
3. Gather information systematically
4. Cross-reference and validate findings
5. Synthesize into coherent analysis

### Source Evaluation
- Prefer primary sources when available
- Check publication dates for currency
- Consider source credibility and bias
- Note confidence levels in findings

### Analysis Framework
- Compare and contrast options objectively
- Identify tradeoffs and considerations
- Highlight uncertainties and gaps
- Provide clear recommendations with rationale
